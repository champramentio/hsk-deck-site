Privacy

Data Deletion

Effective date: June 15, 2026

HSK Deck does not require an account and does not store personal information on our servers. This page explains how to delete local data stored by the app on your device.

Delete Local App Data

Learning progress is stored locally on your device. You can delete local data by uninstalling HSK Deck or clearing app storage in Android settings.

  1. Open your device Settings.
  2. Go to Apps and choose HSK Deck.
  3. Open Storage.
  4. Choose Clear storage or Clear data.

Purchase Records

Google Play may keep purchase records for billing, refunds, tax, fraud prevention, and account history. Those records are managed by Google Play, not by HSK Deck.

Account Deletion

HSK Deck currently does not offer account creation. Because there is no HSK Deck account, there is no app account to delete. If a future version adds account features, this page and the Privacy Policy will be updated with account deletion instructions.

What Is Deleted

Clearing app storage or uninstalling the app deletes local learning progress and preferences stored on the device. It does not delete purchase records maintained by Google Play.

Request Help

If you need help understanding what data is stored by the app, contact us through the Support page.

Google Play note

This page is useful if Google asks for data deletion instructions or a support URL.
