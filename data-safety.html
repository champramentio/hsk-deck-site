Google Play

Data Safety Summary

Effective date: June 15, 2026

This page summarizes HSK Deck’s current data practices in plain language. It is provided to keep the website, Privacy Policy, and Google Play disclosures consistent.

Current App Behavior

  • No account is required.
  • No ads are shown.
  • No advertising trackers are used.
  • No analytics SDK is currently used.
  • Learning progress is stored locally on your device.
  • Optional premium purchases are processed by Google Play Billing.

Data Collected by HSK Deck

Based on the current app behavior, HSK Deck itself does not collect user data on our servers and does not share user data with other companies or organizations.

Local Data

The app stores local learning data, such as review progress, quiz progress, preferences, and premium unlock state. This local data is used to provide app functionality and remains on your device.

Purchase Processing

Google Play handles billing for the optional lifetime unlock. HSK Deck does not receive or store payment card details.

Security Practices

HSK Deck does not transmit personal learning data to our servers. If future online features are added, the app and this website will be updated to explain what data is transmitted and how it is protected.

Data Deletion

You can delete local app data by uninstalling HSK Deck or clearing app storage in Android settings. See Data Deletion.

Disclosure note

The Play Console Data Safety form must always match the actual app behavior and the Privacy Policy.
